FAQs

  • Is registration on website free?
    Yes. Registration is free for both job seekers and job advertisers.
  • How do I Apply for jobs?
    • First create an account in our website.
    • Update your profile UpToDate.
    • Make sure that you give your active mobile number and email id.
    • Upload your resume.
    • If you find a job interesting apply for it.
    • Then sit back and relax. The company will call you if they find your resume interesting.
    • For better results, always apply for more number of jobs.
  • Do I need to pay to get a job here?
    No. Our website is a free website for job seekers and advertisers. If you come across any company who asks you to pay, kindly report them immediately or send your complaints to our mail id.
  • In what format should I upload my resume and other certificates if any?
    You can upload your documents either in .pdf format or in a word document format. Images can be uploaded in .jpeg or .jpg or .png formats.
  • Can I control who can view my profile?
    Yes. That can be done in settings. Go to settings and then to My Profile. There you have option to control the privacy of your profile.
  • If I applied for two jobs at a time and got offer from one company and I accepted it. If the other company approaches me, what should I do?
    Explain to them that you have been offered with a job in some other company and tell them that you are no longer interested in their company. Never ignore them and don’t keep them waiting.
  • Can I delete or deactivate my account?
    Yes. You can delete or deactivate your account. Go to your profile and then to settings. In settings you can find Account settings. There options for deleting and deactivating will be available.
  • I forgot the password of my professional account. What to do?
    If you have forgotten your account password, go to the login page and enter the registered email id. After that, click the “Forgot my password” link below the password textbox. Now a “Temporary link to change the password” will be sent to your registered email id. Using that link, create a new password and login to your account.
  • After applying for a job, where can I find the status of my application?
    To find the status of your application, go to your profile and then to my applications. There you can find all the jobs that you have applied so far along with the application status.
  • Can I apply to more than one job at a time?
    The various status are as follows.
    • Applied – You have applied for a job.
    • Seen – The recruiter has seen your application.
    • In touch – The recruiter has started a communication with you. Try checking the chat box or your mail (including junk and spam folder) to see the message from the recruiter.
    • Selected – You have been selected by the recruiter for the applied job.
    • Not selected – Your application was rejected by the recruiter.
    • Expired – The recruiter has closed the openings for the job and no longer accepts any application.
  • What are some of the safety measures to be followed when I apply for a job in professionals website?
    1. Never share your personal information such as your bank account details, credit/debit card details with the recruiters. 2. Our website never asks anyone to pay for getting you interviewed or to give you a job. So be alert. 3. Check the reviews of the company before applying for any job. 4. Always make sure that you get your offer letter before joining any company. 5. Research about a company thoroughly before applying for any job. 6. Check the terms and conditions of the company before signing the offer letter. 7. Get a clear understanding about how the company works before joining.
